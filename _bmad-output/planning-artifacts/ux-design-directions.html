Adelaide Railway Station

Your guide to navigating the station with confidence. Know what to expect before you arrive.

Section 01

Main Concourse

Sound Light Crowds

"The main concourse can be overwhelming during peak hours. Plan to arrive during quieter periods if possible."

Sound Environment

PA announcements every 2-3 minutes. The high ceilings create echoes that can amplify sounds. Consider noise-cancelling headphones during peak hours.

Lighting Conditions

Large skylights create bright conditions, especially around midday. Fluorescent backup lighting is present throughout. Sunglasses may help on sunny days.
