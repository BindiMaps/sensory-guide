Adelaide Railway Station

North Terrace, Adelaide SA 5000 · Updated January 2026

About this guide

This guide describes what you might see, hear, and experience at each area of the station.

Main Concourse

Sound Light Crowds
Medium

Sound

PA announcements every 2-3 minutes. Echoes due to high ceilings. Consider noise-cancelling headphones during peak hours.

Bright Light

Large skylights create bright conditions midday. Sunglasses may help on sunny days.

Platform Access

Sound
High

Loud Sounds

Train announcements are loud and sudden. Noise-cancelling headphones recommended.

Ticketing Area

Crowds
Low

Crowds

Can get busy during peak times. Self-service machines available.

Restrooms

Sound Smells
Medium

Hand Dryers

Automatic dryers are loud. Paper towels also available.

Sensory Level Key

Low — Calm
Medium — Some activity
High — May be overwhelming
