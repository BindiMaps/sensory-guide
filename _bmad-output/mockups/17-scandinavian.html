Sensory Guide

Adelaide Railway Station

North Terrace, Adelaide SA 5000 · Updated January 2026

This guide describes what you might see, hear, and experience at each area of the station. Select a section to view detailed sensory information.

Sound

PA announcements every 2–3 minutes. The high ceilings create echoes. Consider noise-cancelling headphones during peak hours.

Bright Light

Large skylights create bright conditions around midday. Fluorescent backup lighting throughout.

Crowds

Busiest 7:30–9:00am and 4:30–6:00pm weekdays. Quieter mid-morning and early afternoon.

Crowds

Can get busy during peak times. Self-service machines available.

Screens

Ticket machines have bright screens. Staff counter has softer lighting.

Loud Sounds

Train announcements are loud and sudden. Noise-cancelling headphones strongly recommended.

Sound

Automatic hand dryers can be very loud. Paper towels also available.

Smells

Cleaning products used regularly. Some air freshener scent.

Food Smells

Multiple vendors create a mix of cooking smells. Strongest during lunch.

Seating

Open seating can be crowded at mealtimes. Quieter tables at the edges.
